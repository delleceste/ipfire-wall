The forward block

The packets in the forward block have arrived from a remote host and are directed towards another machine, not to our one. They have passed the pre routing phase and, if we allow them, they will jump to the post routing stage. Otherwise, if we deny the connection, no forward action will happen for the packet.

Here you can filter the network packets that are about to be forwarded through your computer.

  • One can block a connection adding a forward denial rule, or;
  • forward a connection adding a forward permission rule.
