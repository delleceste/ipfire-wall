Notify a packet/connection

If this check is ticked, a popup will notify that this rule has just been applied. The popup will normally present a summary of the packet just received and filtered according to its associated rule.

The popup will appear over the iqfire-wall system tray icon and will be shown for a period of time tunable in the System Tray tab in this window, in the section Notify a packet matching a firewall rule.

If the mouse enters the popup window, it will not be hidden, and will be closed only by pressing the appropriate close link.

Only one popup is visible at a time, and if you want to avoid to lock the information displayed at a certain instant, click on the locklink in the upper part of the popup itself.

Actually, the popup for the matching packets notification is made up of an html page.
