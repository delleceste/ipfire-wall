Output blocked packets

The output blocked packets over total packets processed curve represents the percentage of the output packets blocked through the firewall over the total packets processed.
This curve is represented in red and its line is continuous.

Move your mouse over the graph to watch the curve details (date and percentage).

Note

The time domain is divided into small periods and the statistics are calculated over each period. If you view the plot as steps, then each step represents the percentage of packets blocked/dropped y axis over the time slot represented by the step width in the time axis.
