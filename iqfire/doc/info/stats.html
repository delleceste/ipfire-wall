Statistics

The statistics window represents with great detail the activity of the firewall over time.

Two visualizations are available:

  • A plot representing the percentage of packets blocked or allowed for each direction over time
  • An html formatted page, which sums up the statistics in a numeric form.

Note that, while the plot starts gathering the information at the moment of the startup of the iqFirewall user interface, the textual page tracks the behaviour of the firewall since the kernel module of ipfire-wall is loaded. This typically means at computer startup.

Beware of the different information that may contain the plot and the textual summary then!
