Allow an unprivileged user to insert his rules

Check this box if you want to allow a normal user to insert his own rules. Remember that his rules will have a lower priority with respect to administrator's, i.e. will be applied after administrator's. Moreover, the root rules cannot be modified or deleted by a normal user.

This is a special feature of iqfire-wall and it is usually safe to enable it, of course the administrator having safely configured the firewall first.
