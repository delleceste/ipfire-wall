iqFirewall presentation

This is iqfirewall information section.
In this corner you will find some useful piece of information about the contents of the firewall window currently displayed.

  • You can hide/show this section clicking on the Sidebar Contents toolbar, on the i icon;
  • moving your mouse over the window items will provide you useful information about them.
  • Usually, bringing the mouse pointer outside the firewall window and then moving it again on the main widget of the window itself, will display the generic information on the section currently shown (this page is an example).
  • Scroll the mouse over an item to scroll the contents of the help or the info text browsers!
  • NOTE: the information text is updated with a delay of a few milliseconds after the mouse enters a widget, to avoid continuous changes of the information contents during the user mouse movements over a window. The delay is tunable in the configuration window, under the paths page.
