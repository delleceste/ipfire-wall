The console

This is the iqFirewall console. It is the kde konsole, which is loaded at startup as a part of the graphical interface. It shares the profile of your kde console (fonts, colors, number of lines, and so on). So, if you like to change such settings, open a konsole from the menu K -> system -> konsole and change its settings. Saving them as the global profile will result in having the iqFirewall console consistent with your kde konsole.
