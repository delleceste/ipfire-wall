A circular gauge as firewall activity indicator

The circular gauge presents two needles: one is green and the other is red. The needles are arrow terminated: the red one represents the number of packets filtered and blocked per time unit, the green one the filtered and accepted packets.

The system tray icon works like and analogue dashboard, configurable through the other available paramters: Show needle arrows, Mean Adjust Factor, Relative length of the Accept Needle and Relative length of the Block Needle,

The level of the needles is proportional to the number of packets processed in a certain amount of time compared with the mean of the number of packets processed during all the firewall activity.
