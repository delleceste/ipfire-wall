Rule name

The rule name briefly describes your firewall rule.
You can type a name made up of any type of character, and long at most 50 characters. Note that not all the characters are allowed, so if you notice that you cannot type a character inside the line edit, do not worry: it is one of those not allowed!

Hint:
Associating a good and significant name to a rule is useful to identify and recognize the rule itself without having to look at all its fields. an
