Tune the length of the allowed packets needle

Adjust this factor from 0.0 to 1.0 to tune the length of the green needle indicating the allowed packets flow.

Having the red and the green needles drawn with different lengths allows easier distinguishing the two indicators in case they are overlapping (i.e. indicating the same value).

A value of 0.0 means no green needle, a value of 1.0 draws a needle long the full available length of the radius of the System Tray Icon.
