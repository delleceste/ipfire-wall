Forward implicitly allowed packets

The forward implicitly allowed packets over total packets processed curve represents the percentage of the forward packets implicitly allowed through the firewall over the total packets processed.
This curve is represented in green and its line is dashed.

Move your mouse over the graph to watch the curve details (date and percentage).

Note

The time domain is divided into small periods and the statistics are calculated over each period. If you view the plot as steps, then each step represents the percentage of packets implicitly allowed/dropped y axis over the time slot represented by the step width in the time axis.
